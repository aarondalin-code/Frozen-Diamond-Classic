Game Format
Core structure and scoring rules.
Time Limit
No new inning can start after 1:40. Hard stop at 1:55. Score reverts to the last completed inning.

All games are 6 innings unless shortened by time limit or run rule.
Home Team
Pre-game coin flip.
Mercy Rule
10-run mercy rule after losing team bats in the 4th inning.

Pitching
Limits, mound visits, and player safety.
Pitch Count Limit
Pitchers are limited to 75 total pitches for the day/tournament. Strongly encouraged to not pitch the same pitcher in back-to-back games.

2 per pitcher. Pitcher must be replaced on the 2nd visit.
