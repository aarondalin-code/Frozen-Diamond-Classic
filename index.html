Frozen Diamond Classic

Tournament Central
Tournament Day
Saturday, March 21, 2026

Tournament Overview

The Frozen Diamond Classic is a one-day 9U baseball tournament designed to provide a competitive and fun indoor experience during the winter. Teams are encouraged to hang between games. All teams are guaranteed three games.

Tournament Details

  • Date: Saturday, March 21, 2026
  • Location: CenterCourt – 1080 Spruce Street, Lawrence
  • CenterCourt Lawrence Venue Page
  • Tournament T-Shirt Order Form
  • Teams: 8 teams, invite only — Town level A or strong B
  • Rules: Standard 9U (see Rules page)
  • Scoring: GameChanger Tournament — “Frozen Diamond Classic 2026”

Facility Information

  • Climate: It can get cool — dress appropriately (long sleeve under jersey recommended).
  • Chairs: No bleachers available — bring your own chairs or blankets.
  • Teams may bring coolers with snacks and drinks.
  • Picnic-style tables available between games.
