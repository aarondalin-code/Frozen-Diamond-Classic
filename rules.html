Frozen Diamond Classic

Tournament Central

Official Tournament Rules

Field & Game Structure

  • Field Size: 46’ pitching distance / 60’ base paths
  • Innings: 6 innings unless shortened by time limit or mercy rule
  • Home Team: Determined by pre-game coin flip
  • Mercy Rule: 10 runs after losing team bats in the 4th inning
  • Max Runs per Inning: No maximum (subject to discussion)
  • Dome Rules: Local house rules – TBD

Time Limits

  • No new inning may begin after 1:40
  • Hard stop at 1:55
  • Score reverts to last completed inning if game ends mid-inning

Roster & Player Eligibility

  • If teams are concerned about pitching limits, teams can expand roster size with other 9U travel players from the same town/organization (if applicable).
  • 10U players are strictly prohibited from playing.
  • Pre-approval from league director required.

Pitching Rules

  • Pitch Limit: 75 total pitches per pitcher for the day/tournament
  • Strongly encouraged not to pitch the same pitcher in back-to-back games
  • Mound Visits: 2 per pitcher; pitcher must be replaced on 2nd visit

Lineup & Batting

  • Roster Batting: All uniform players must bat
  • Late arrival goes to the bottom of the order
  • Batting out of order: Batter out after 1 pitch
  • No penalty for injury or early departure
  • Defensive Substitutions: Standard rules apply; unlimited re-entry

Base Running & Field Play

  • Courtesy Runner: May be used for catcher or pitcher of record at any time. Runner must be the player who made the last batted out.
  • Bunting: Allowed. No fake bunt slash hitting.
  • Lead-Offs: No leading off until pitched ball reaches or passes the batter. One warning per team; subsequent violations = runner out.
  • Stealing: All bases allowed. Pitcher pickoffs not allowed; catcher pickoffs allowed.
  • Sliding: Feet first only
  • Dropped Third Strike: Not in effect
  • Infield Fly Rule: Not in effect

Equipment

  • Bats: USA bats only (no USSSA or BBCOR)
  • Cleats: Rubber only (no metal cleats)
  • Required Equipment: Protective cup and heart guard

Sportsmanship

  • No chanting when pitcher is in windup
