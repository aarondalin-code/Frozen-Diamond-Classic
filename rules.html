Tournament Central

Official Tournament Rules

Field & Game Structure

  • Field Size: 46’ pitching distance / 60’ base paths
  • Innings: 6 innings unless shortened by time limit or mercy rule
  • Home Team: Determined by pre-game coin flip
  • Mercy Rule: 10 runs after losing team bats in the 4th inning
  • Max Runs per Inning: No maximum (subject to discussion)

Time Limits

  • No new inning may begin after 1:40
  • Hard stop at 1:55
  • Score reverts to last completed inning if game ends mid-inning

Roster & Player Eligibility

  • Teams concerned about pitching limits may expand roster with other 9U travel players from the same town/organization (if applicable)
  • 10U players are strictly prohibited
  • League director pre-approval required for roster expansion

Pitching Rules

  • 75 pitch maximum per pitcher for the day/tournament
  • Strongly encouraged not to pitch the same pitcher in back-to-back games
  • Mound Visits: 2 per pitcher; pitcher must be replaced on second visit

Batting & Lineup

  • Roster Batting: All uniformed players must bat
  • Late arrival goes to bottom of order
  • Batting out of order: Batter is out after 1 pitch
  • No penalty for injury or early departure

Base Running & Field Play

  • Lead-Offs: No leading off until pitched ball reaches/passes batter
  • One warning per team for early lead; subsequent violations result in runner being called out
  • Stealing: All bases allowed
  • Pitcher pickoffs not allowed; catcher pickoffs allowed
  • Sliding: Feet first only
  • Courtesy Runner: Allowed for catcher or pitcher of record; must be the last batted out

Special Rules

  • Dropped Third Strike rule is not in effect
  • Infield Fly rule is not in effect
  • Bunting allowed; fake bunt slash hitting not allowed

Equipment

  • Bats: USA bats only (no USSSA or BBCOR)
  • Cleats: Rubber only; metal cleats not permitted
  • Required Equipment: Protective cup and heart guard

Sportsmanship

  • No chanting when pitcher is in windup
