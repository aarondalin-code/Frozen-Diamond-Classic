How It Works

You have two options:

  • Option 1 (Recommended): Upload the file directly
    Choose a photo or video from your phone. It will upload securely to the tournament drive.
  • Option 2: Paste a share link
    Use Google Photos, Apple Photos (iCloud link), Google Drive, Dropbox, or YouTube.

Using Apple Photos (iCloud Link)

  1. Open the photo in Photos.
  2. Tap Share.
  3. Select Copy iCloud Link.
  4. Paste the link into the form.

Using Google Photos

  1. Open the photo in Google Photos.
  2. Tap Share.
  3. Select Create link.
  4. Paste the link into the form.

For best display inside the Gallery, uploading the file directly works best.
