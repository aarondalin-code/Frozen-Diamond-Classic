Frozen Diamond Classic

Tournament Central

Tournament Overview

The Frozen Diamond Classic is a one-day 9U baseball tournament designed to provide a competitive and fun indoor experience during the winter. Teams are encouraged to hang between games. All teams are guaranteed three games.

  • Date: Saturday, March 21, 2026
  • Location: CenterCourt – 1080 Spruce Street, Lawrence

  • Teams: 8 teams, invite only — Town level A or strong B
  • Rules: Standard 9U (see Rules page)

Facility Information

  • Climate: It can get cool, so please dress appropriately (players + spectators). Long sleeve under jersey is recommended for players.
  • Chairs/Spectators: No bleachers/chairs — please bring your own chairs/blankets. Space beyond outfield fence is available for spectators.
  • Food: Food trucks will be present. The dome also has vending machines and a water bottle filling station. Teams may bring coolers with snacks/drinks.
  • There is space/area for teams to gather between games.
  • CenterCourt seating/tables: Picnic-style tables available for eating between games.

Off-Site Food

  • Wendy’s / Burger King / McDonald’s / WingStop / QuickChek — ~5 minutes away
